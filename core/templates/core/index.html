Marine Life

Fujairah has the most abundant and diverse marine life in the UAE. Our dive sites are not further than 30 minutes drive by boat, they range from 12m - 30m, there are hard corals and soft corals and a variety of Indian ocean reef fishes, Whalesharks also frequent our shores.

Located On The Beach

Located directly in front of Snoopy Island allows us the best shore diving in the UAE. We are based in Sandy Beach hotel and Resort on the east coast of the UAE, the Fujairah coastline offers the best diving in the UAE with temperate to warm waters all year around.

Boat Diving

We run two boat trips per day, our boat is dedicated for diving with all safety equipment and kit racks. Our boats cater for no more than 12 divers, each dive trip has a dedicated Divemaster on board who will guide you around our favorite dive sites.

Family Environment

Being based in Sandy Beach Hotel and Resort provides our divers with the perfect family environment. Divers can head out for a day of diving on the boat or from shore while their families can enjoy the swimming pool or beach facilities.

Service Excellence

Being a 5-star PADI IDC Centre means we adhere to the latest training standards and teaching techniques. All our dive guides and Instructors are PADI professionals who care for individual diver needs and the environment.

Serviced Equipment

We offer a full range of scuba diving equipment for rental, we also offer technical diving facilities with Nitrox and oxygen. All of our equipment is regularly serviced and maintained to ensure diver comfort and safety.
