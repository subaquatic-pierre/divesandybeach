Check It Out
Located On The Beach

Located directly inforront of snoopy sialnd awards us the best shore diving in the UAE. We are based in Sandy Beach hotel and resort on the east coast of the UAE, the fujairah coastline offers the best diving in the UAE with temperate to warm waters all year around.

Marine Life

Fujairah has the most abundent and diverse marine life in the UAE. Our dive sites are not futher than 30min by boat, ranging from 12m - 30m there are hard corals and soft corals and a variety of indain ocean reef fishes, whalesharks frequent our shores

Boat Diving

We run two boat trips pper day, our boat is dedicated for diving with all safety equipment and kit racks. Our boats caters for no more than 12 divers, each dive trip has a dedicated divemaster on board who will guide you through our favourite dive sites.

Family Environment

Being based in sandy beach hotel and resort provides our divers with the perfect family environment. Divers can head out for a day of diving on the boat or from shore and their families can enjoy the swiming pool or beach facilities.

Service Excellence

Beign a 5-star PADI IDC centre means we adhere to the latest training standards and teaching techniques. All our Diveguids and Instructors are PADI profesionals who care for individual diver needs.

Serviced Equimpment

We offer a full range of scuba diving equipment for rental, we have tech diving facilities with nitrox and oxygen. All of our equipment is regulary serviced and mainted to the highest quality to ensure diver comfort and safety.
