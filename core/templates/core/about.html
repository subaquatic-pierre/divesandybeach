About Us

We’re a PADI 5* IDC Resort, located within the Marine National Park directly in front of Snoopy Island. Sandy Beach Dive Centre prides itself on excellent customer service & training standards with a family environment where all divers feel welcome. There are daily diving trips & courses, to Dive Snoopy Island and other sites. We offer a full range of scuba diving services including Nitrox & Technical diving facilities. Snoopy Island is the only shore dive along the coast Fujairah coastline, it boasts abundant marine life including Blacktip Reef sharks and Resident Green Turtles . We’re based in the Sandy Beach Hotel & Resort, which is the longest operating resort in the area. Our instructors follow the latest training standards & the most cutting-edge teaching techniques and all of our equipment & dive boats are regularly serviced to ensure a safe and enjoyable diving experience.
