About Us

Learn more about Sandy Beach Dive Centre

We're on the beach

We’re based in the Sandy Beach Hotel & Resort, which is the longest operating resort in the area. Our instructors follow the latest training standards and the most cutting-edge teaching techniques and all of our equipment and dive boats are regularly serviced to ensure a safe and enjoyable diving experience.

5 Star PADI IDC

We’re a PADI 5* IDC Resort, located within the Marine National Park directly in front of Snoopy Island. Sandy Beach Dive Centre prides itself on excellent customer service and training standards with a family environment where all divers feel welcome. There are daily diving trips & courses, to Dive Snoopy Island and other sites. We offer a full range of scuba diving services including Nitrox and Technical diving facilities. Snoopy Island is the only shore dive along the coast Fujairah coastline, it boasts abundant marine life including Blacktip Reef sharks and Resident Green Turtles.
